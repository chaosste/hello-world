MSc. project · University of Exeter

EntheoGen focuses on how entheogens, medications, and other psychoactive substances interact in real‑world ceremonial, clinical, and intentional use – not generic "recreational drug" summaries.

We grade each interaction by risk, mechanism, and evidence tier, to support facilitators, clinicians, and informed users in making safer decisions.
